How can I help?

Bees are very important for human life.


Bees are the ones that pollinate our plants (over 80% of them!), which means that they help plants reproduce.
They do this when they get pollen and nectar from male flowers, some of the pollen sticks to bee's hairs. When they go to the next flower, some of the pollen drops onto the female reproductive organ of the flower.
But, bees are under threat of major extinction. One in every four bee species in North America is on the danger of becoming extinct.
